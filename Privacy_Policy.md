
# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BacCard ("we," "us," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our educational flashcard application.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data
- **Flashcard interactions**: We store your progress, difficulty ratings (easy, medium, hard), and study habits locally on your device
- **Favorites**: Cards you mark as favorites are stored locally
- **Study history**: Your classification of cards and repetition patterns

### Data Storage Location
All personal data is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. We do not collect or store your personal data on any external servers.

## How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally:
- We do not transmit your personal information to any servers
- We do not share your data with third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising purposes
- Your learning progress remains private to your device

## Data Sharing

We **do not share** any of your personal information with third parties, including:
- No data is sent to external servers
- No information is shared with advertisers is sold or transferred to other organizations

## Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational purposes and is suitable for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information:
- All data remains on your device
- No internet connection is required for core functionality
- Data is stored securely using the device's built-in storage system

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting. These services may collect information to identify you:
- Crash reports (if implemented)
- Usage analytics (if implemented)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
